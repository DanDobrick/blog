Why a Links Page?

I really like the idea of small websites/personal blogs, and I think that the web would be a better place if there were more sites like that. One good way of ensuring that more people see these small sites is to create links between them; to quote a blogpost about the small web:

Whenever you stumble upon an interesting thought on another site, write about it and link to it. Not only is it respectful to link to the person you quoted, but hyperlinks are also the magic force holding the Web together.
So here are some links that I found interesting and I hope you enjoy them! I've included a link to the blog and sometimes an article or two that I enjoyed.

Disclaimer: I enjoyed the linked articles and encourage you to explore these sites, but I cannot guarantee their content. Please don't take my linking to them as an endorsement of their entire site and/or the views espoused therein.
