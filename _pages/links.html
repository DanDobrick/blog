I really like the idea of small websites/personal blogs, and I think that the web would be a better place if there were more of them. One good way of ensuring that more people see these small sites is to create links between them, so here are some links that I found intersesting and I hope you enjoy them! I've included a link to the blog and an article or two that I enjoyed.

Disclaimer: I enjoyed the article linked and encourage you to explore these sites, but I did not vet them beyond reading an article or two and cannot guarantee their content. Don't take my linking to them as an endorsement of their entire site and/or the views espoused therein.
